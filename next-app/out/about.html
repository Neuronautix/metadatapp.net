The Story behind MAAP

After 15 years in preclinical research, Damien felt the lack of a proper tool to accurately and efficiently organize experimental metadata. MAAP was born to fix that gap.

Our Vision for the Future of Science

We envision scientific data that is transparent, shareable, and reproducible. By fostering open standards and collaboration, MAAP helps researchers spend more time on discovery and less on wrangling spreadsheets.
